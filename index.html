WHAT YOU GET
Everything you need to train like a pro
Three complete training programs in one app. Step-by-step guided workouts with timers, progression tracking, and AI video coaching.

Stickhandling

29 drills across 6 sessions. Daily hands, fundamentals, game day prep, moves and dekes, puck protection, and speed hands. Built on methods from Lance Pitlick, Sean Skinner, and Hendrikx Hockey.

Shooting

20 drills across 5 sessions. Shot foundation, wrist shot mastery, snap shot, slap shot, and game scoring. Informed by Tim Turk (NHL shooting coach), Dwayne Blais (Detroit Red Wings), and Auston Matthews' mechanics.

Mobility

31 exercises across 7 sessions. Daily baseline, morning wake-up, bedtime wind-down, and game-specific flows. Based on Matt Nichol, Ben Prentiss, and Rob Dombrowski methodologies.

Guided Workouts

No guessing. Tap a session and the app walks you through each drill one at a time. Built-in timers for timed drills. Details available if you need the step-by-step breakdown.

AI Video Coaching

The biggest advancement in player development is here. Record yourself doing any drill and AI analyzes your mechanics in real time — giving you the same form corrections a $200/hour skills coach would. No scheduling, no rink required.

Progress Tracking

XP system, streak tracking, and completion rings for every session. See your progress across all three disciplines at a glance. Stay consistent, get better.

THE METHOD
Built on real coaching, not random drills
Every drill in EDGE is informed by the same methodologies used to train NHL players. We studied the best coaches and distilled their systems into guided off-ice workouts.
Tim Turk
NHL Shooting Coach
Worked with the Canadiens, Lightning, Hurricanes, Coyotes, and Flames. Trained Bedard, Gallagher, Hedman, Marner, Kadri, and Mangiapane. His 3-phase shooting system is the foundation of our shooting program.
Dwayne Blais
Detroit Red Wings Skill Development
Hockey Canada certified, founder of Total Package Hockey and the National Skill Development Association. His progressive approach — stationary to skating to game speed — shapes how every session flows.
Auston Matthews
Shooting Mechanics Study
We analyzed Matthews' signature techniques — the angle change, shooting in stride, the backhand pull — and built drills that teach you the same mechanics the best goal scorer in the world uses.
